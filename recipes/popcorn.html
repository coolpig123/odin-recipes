popcorn

Description

this web page will teach you how to make a popcorn!

Ingredients

  • oil
  • corn seeds
  • cooking pot
  • bowl
  • salt (optional)

Steps

  1. put the oil and the pot and turn on the stove
  2. wait for the pot to be hot
  3. pour the corn seeds into the pot
  4. wait for the seeds the finish popping
  5. once they will stop popping put them in the bowl and add the salt!
