tea

Description

this web page will teach you how to make a cup of tea!

Ingredients

  • cup of tea
  • suger (optional)
  • water
  • spoon
  • tea leaves

Steps

  1. boil the water
  2. put in the cup the suger and the tea leaves
  3. pour the water to the cup and mix with the spoon
  4. put the spoon away or not and enjoy drinking your tea!
